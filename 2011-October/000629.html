> Looking at the list of boost math libraries, I see there's now a 
> libboost_math_tr1f and libboost_math_c99f. Maybe adding these to your 
> makefile helps? Just edit the make.config file and add those to the 
> LIBS variable. It may also make a difference whether you add those to 
> the front or the back of the list of libraries...

Yes, bingo! In fact, the winning step is to do the ./configure and then 
change manually the make.config file so that it references only the 
*boost-*f libs, as shown below:

LIBS          += zeep bz2 z boost_program_options boost_thread 
boost_filesystem boost_regex boost_math_c99f boost_math_tr1f 
boost_system archive m

This compiles and produces the mrs-ws executable which I can now confirm 
it has not got the 302 bug, so you might like to review it for your next 
commit! Cheers!

To confirm, RHEL6, with libboost 1.47, libzeep 2.6.1 on svn trunk rev 
1381 gets rid of the problem.
