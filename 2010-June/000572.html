Hi,

It seems that some queries that contain parenthesis and an AND operator are
evaluated as syntactically incorrect. For example, these work fine:

lysosyme AND lysozyme
lysosyme & lysozyme
lysosyme OR lysozyme
lysosyme | lysozyme

But if I surround it by parenthesis, the first two produce errors:

Expected word but found AND (lysosyme *AND* lysozyme)
Expected word but found AND (lysosyme *&* lysozyme)

The ones with OR still work fine, even surrounded by parenthesis.

I wonder if this is a feature or a bug... :-)

Also the query containing a dollar sign gives me an error:

HS$BRCA2_02
Expected word but found '$' HS*$*BRCA2_02
