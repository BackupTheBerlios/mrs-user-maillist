Some remarks. You mention your machine has 'only' 6 Gb of RAM. What  
is the value of your MRS_PARTS? Since the distribution of records is  
so uneven, you end up with some very large parts and memory may  
become a bottleneck. I've seen memory usage up to 3 Gb, and if you  
have two of those at the same time the machine will likely start  
swapping which is bad.

On cmbi8 we have 8 Gb of RAM and MRS_PARTS=32, maybe you should use  
32 as well?
