Hi Maarten and friends,

Here is the ls -lt of my recently indexed MRS3 directory:
Times taken for sections 1 and 2 of embl_release are totally atypical, 
as during the same period, the computer was very actively installing and 
using iprscan, as well as getting large quantities of data over its 
internal Ethernet card. From about 20:00, MRS indexing was essentially 
the only job going on. Thus we can say that on this machine, indexing 
the complete embl_release took about 28-30 hours. Time taken for 
indexing the updates is correct, as at that time, the computer was 
essentially idle.
This is a first generation dual-Opteron box with 6 gigabyte RAM and a 
SATA  RAID5 array mounted on a 3-Ware raid controller. I have the 
feeling that this brand of controller is slower than its Vortex 
counterpart I have on the other box I mentioned some days ago.

Maarten, your manual about MRS scripting is excellent. Thank you for this.

Greetings,
Robert
