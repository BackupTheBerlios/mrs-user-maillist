Hi,

I get the impression that the current way of distributing MRS is sub  
optimal...

And so I'm thinking about a binary distribution. The options are:

- .deb Debian packages, also usable in Ubuntu.
- .rpm RedHat packages, might be installable in a Debian environment  
as well.

Alternatives are adding MRS to e.g. NetBSD's pkgsrc. But I'm afraid  
that's too much trouble for most. And the option of adding MRS to  
FreeBSD's port collection which requires you to use FreeBSD as an  
operating system is even more undesirable (although that would be my  
preference...).

So I'd like to hear your votes please.
