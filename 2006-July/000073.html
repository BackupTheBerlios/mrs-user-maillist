Op 26-jul-2006, om 16:20 heeft Dr. Andrea Schafferhans het volgende  
geschreven:

> What I expected is that after setting the DATABASES variable,
> make all would only make those databases. (Do you agree with that
> expectation?) However, make all depends on DBALLFLAGS which in turn is
> generated from ALL_DATABASES. Therefore, it ignores the setting of
> DATABASES.

Makes sense, absolutely.

I'll add another variable DBFLAGS, analogous to the DBALLFLAGS, like  
this:

DBFLAGS =		$(addprefix $(FLAGSDIR), $(DATABASES))

And then all can depend on ${DBFLAGS:=.done}

Should work as you expected.
