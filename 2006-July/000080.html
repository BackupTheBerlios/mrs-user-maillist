You could troubleshoot the FTP module problem by mirroring the 
problematic dirs or files using a simple client (wget for instance). If 
you have the diskspace in various workstations or servers, run then an 
FTP server there and point your download paths on your local FTP. I have 
seen similar problems in other dirs of the the EBI FTP server.

If a wget -c ftp://ftp.ebi.ac.uk/pub/databases/intepro/match.xml.gz
fails consistently from your location, you have your answer.

If the FTP connection to EBI is the problem for whatever reason, in the 
process of finding another mirror site, you could try biomirror:

ftp://bio-mirror.net/biomirror//interpro/

Maybe this will help you.

Out of curiosity, and because it was a previous issue raised in the 
list, is anyone playing with torrents at the moment?
