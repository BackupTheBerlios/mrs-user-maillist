I think I know what the problem is, it's on springbok, right?

You have 'sprot|trembl' as an alias for uniprot. This won't work, I  
believe there's a bug somewhere. What you should do is replace 'sprot| 
trembl' with 'sprot+trembl'. The difference is that the latter is a  
simple concatenation of the two databanks while the first is an  
update. Uniprot is not an update collection like EMBL Release plus  
EMBL Updates is. There's also a performance hit for update databanks  
since the amount of work that needs to be done is more.

And then, the order is also important. Suppose you consider sprot and  
trembl an update combination, then sprot is the update for trembl and  
the order should be 'trembl|sprot'. (The output of trembl is filtered  
by sprot.)

but don't remember what I saw then. :-)
