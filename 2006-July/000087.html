> And one more question, to Maarten now. Can you inform the folks about
> how to get the 3D-applet installed for pdb entry viewing ? Not urgent,
> but so nice to have !

That's a bit tricky. At first you have to install jmol (http:// 
jmol.sourceforge.net/) and it has to reside in a location where  
apache can find it. The paths where mrs thinks it is installed are  
defined in the Format_pdb.pm file.

If you have it installed in a different location you have to fiddle  
with the Alias statements in your httpd.conf to allow apache to find  
the right files. Having a close look at access_log and error_log  
while reloading a pdb entry in your webbrowser will reveal what needs  
to go where.

Good luck,

-maarten
