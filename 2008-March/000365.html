> Hi,
>
> Someone mailed me/the list to tell that mrsws-clustal would crash at
> launch with a segmentation violation. I've found out that this can
> happen when there was no clustalw installed when the
> /usr/local/etc/mrs-config.xml file was generated. The <clustalw-exe>
> entry would be left empty and I just found out you get a nil pointer at
> a location I didn't expect from libxml2...
>
> Anyway, if you want to use mrsws-clustal you have to have clustalw
> installed and have to enter it's path in the mrs-config.xml file. I'll
> fix the crash nevertheless.
