Just to let you know that you should probably register another URL than 
the 'rsync://ftp.ncbi.nih.gov/repository/OMIM/ in the databank.info'. 
NCBI no longer maintains the OMIM rsync/ftp repository. OMIM has moved 
to omim.org and there is anonymous FTP now on:

ftp://anonymous@grcf.jhmi.edu/OMIM/
