The make.config file Uli sent today made me realize that the 
configure.pl script isn't perfect yet.

If you look at the make.config file you'll notice more often than not 
that the OPT variable is left empty. If you have a very recent gcc you 
can put '-march=native' here to have the best optimisation for your 
computer. Especially on 32 bit machines this will result in quite a 
performance increase.
