I definately support this idea
both syntax should co-exist
people may be mind-oriented as you mentioned regarding google behaviour

but most of old users SRS-experienced wish to keep their ?old? mind 
orientation.

secondly, I think it is not bad to have explicit queries working as 
expected,
lots of people understand better
coffee AND milk BUT NOT sugar

and might wonder what :
coffee +milk -sugar means (is that coffee light 0%?)
is it different of
coffee +milk ?
