Op 2-okt-2006, om 16:09 heeft Guy Bottu het volgende geschreven:
>
> We recently upgraded our Perl libraries (we have now SOAP::Lite  
> version
> 0.66) and, as a consequence, a strange misbehaviour has appeared.

Well, there's a warning and that warning can be removed by replacing  
the line:

my $soap = SOAP::Lite->uri($ns_url)->proxy($url);

with:

my $soap = SOAP::Lite->new(proxy => $url);
$soap->default_ns($ns_url);
