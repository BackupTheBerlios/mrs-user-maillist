Maarten:

   I solved the problems and I successfully installed MRS 3.0.1 on a Fedora Core
6(64bits).

   The solution was to install the right libraries. I the case of gSoap, only
version 2.7.7 was suitable. It seems each version has different fuction
prototypes.


   Here are the libraries I used:

   	boost-1.33.1-11.fc6.x86_64.rpm

	boost-devel-1.33.1-11.fc6.x86_64.rpm

        gsoap_2.7.7.tar.gz


   Thanks!
