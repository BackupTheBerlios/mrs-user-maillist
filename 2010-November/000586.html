dbEST records do not have a referenced Entrez-index. The closest thing 
in the MRS 4 index is a genbank_id. So, IMHO what you need to do is to:

1)Locate Genbank accessions or IDs from the Entrez Gene.
2)For each record with a Genbank ID/Accession query dbEST using the 
'genbank_id' or 'genbank_acc' indices.

I normally also cross reference that from Unigene as well.
