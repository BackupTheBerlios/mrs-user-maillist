	Dear Maarten,

I have a small suggestion about a cosmetic issue. A search in PROSITE 
documentation for kringle yields :

*Fibronectin type-I domain signature and profile**
*Kringle domain signature and profile*
*C-type lectin domain signature and profile**
*WSC domain profile*

The stars come from the ****** lines around the title  as well as from 
the text (hence their unpredictable number). I think you can get rid of
them by replacing in prosite_doc.pm line 95
elsif ($line =~ m/\*\s*(.+?)\s*\*/) {
by
elsif ($line =~ m/\*\s+(.+?)\s+\*/) {
