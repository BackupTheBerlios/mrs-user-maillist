Op 16-feb-2007, om 15:27 heeft Guy Bottu het volgende geschreven:
>
> The stars come from the ****** lines around the title  as well as from
> the text (hence their unpredictable number). I think you can get  
> rid of
> them by replacing in prosite_doc.pm line 95
> elsif ($line =~ m/\*\s*(.+?)\s*\*/) {
> by
> elsif ($line =~ m/\*\s+(.+?)\s+\*/) {

Your suggestion will probably work, but I'm not so sure there will  
always be whitespace between the title and the surrounding stars. And  
thus I changed the line to:

elsif ($line =~ m/\*\s*([^*].+?)\s*\*/) {

And that seems to work fine.
