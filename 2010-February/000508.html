Well.... maybe not. Point is, I only created support for blasting 
protein sequences. I never bothered to create one of the other blast 
options. As a result, I don't store the DNA sequences of Genbank entries 
separately and thus I don't have direct support for a fasta export of 
genbank entries.

Now suppose you want to have a fasta output for Genbank, you could 
implement a 'sequence' procedure in the genbank.pm file. For an example 
on how to do this, have a look at embl.pm.
