I have installed mrsweb (and mrs-ws) locally. Finally, I succeeded to add
(Entrez)-Gene databank. But there seem to be some issues with the display. I
wonder where I made a mistake or what step I missed.

For example, this entry (this URL):
http://cbrcgit:8080/mrs-web/entry.do?db=gene&id=33212&query=cone+snail shows
(see also the screenshot) a link to Organism (Drosophila melanogaster) as "
http://cbrcgit:8080/mrs-web/query.do?db=taxonomy&id={$tax_id}" - where
{$tax_id} seems suspicious (and, of course, it does not resolve). The same
is with all pubmed links (and the links are not really visually separated
one from another, as seen on the screenshot):
http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/query.fcgi?db=PubMed&cmd=Retrieve&list_uids={$pmid}
.

I suspect that this is a result of applying the XSLT in the parser script? I
followed the advise (given in this mailing list recently) and uncommented
"use XML::XSLT;" line in the 'gene.pm' file.

I came to this records by making a query "cone snail" and clicking on the
first found record (see another attached screenshot). Also, perhaps
interesting, I have not found the same record at MRS server at CMBI.
