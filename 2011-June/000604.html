Galaxy is a kind of framework to put bioinformatics tools and data 
together in an orderly fashion. You can also extract workflows from the 
analysis you have performed (called history).
It is completely open-source and every tool can be integrated in Galaxy. 
A large community of about 200 people is now developing it.

Good introductions to Galaxy on http://usegalaxy.org

Unfortunately, I do not know exactly what is required to integrate a 
third party data provider. Most tools work by passing an URL to Galaxy 
where Galaxy can then download the data. An explanation on 
https://bitbucket.org/galaxy/galaxy-central/wiki/DataSources


Thanks for the consideration,
Joachim
