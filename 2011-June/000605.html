We have a project to address that. I cannot provide further details at 
this stage as it has not commenced yet (it will after the mid of July) 
but the plan is to integrate MRS as part of our Galaxy installation here 
in Oslo.

This will involve the porting of the mrs-search binary (or a proxy web 
service to talk to an MRS server) and possibly combine it with useful 
EMBOSS/other post processing steps.

For that second part, feel free to suggest steps and/or pipelines that 
are useful to you (MRS search->other apps) in this list. It should not 
be that difficult to achieve, given the glue nature of Galaxy.
